Manufacturing Capabilities

Scotland Manufacturing's vast array of equipment allows us to provide a diversified product line in a highly flexible manufacturing environment. We design and manufacture products for a wide range of industries including filtration, automotive, construction, rail and aerospace. As a "one-stop-shop," our production facility offers value-added services such as welding, assembly and pressure testing.

Scotland Manufacturing has the following
state-of-the-art equipment:
  • High-speed stamping and hand transfer stamping presses
  • Ability to produce deep drawn shells from 2-5/8" to 5" diameter up to 12" tall
  • Brake presses
  • Assembly and test cells
  • Manual hand transfer presses for low volume parts (i.e., service parts)

Flexible Production Capacity

  • Low, medium and high volume capabilities
  • Presses ranging from 110 tons to 1000 tons
  • Value-added services: welding, assembly, pressure testing
Scotland Manufacturing designs, develops and maintains its own tooling for quality assurance on every stamped product.
