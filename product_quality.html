Product Quality

Quality is not just another department at Scotland Manufacturing; it is encouraged in all areas of the organization.

Scotland Manufacturing's Quality Control structure begins with ISO 9001 as its foundation. ISO 9001 provides the framework to develop systems to support customer demands. Through performance metrics, internal and external audits, and formalized management reviews, manufacturing procedures are continually updated and expanded.

Product Quality is achieved through the development of robust processes and detailed inspection procedures. An on-site Quality Assurance Lab uses the latest advanced inspection equipment, while key product features are monitored and controlled using statistical process control (SPC) charts.

An employee-driven Quality Improvement Program accepts ideas for improvement in quality, cost, efficiency, and process development from all areas of the company.

Quality Engineers are involved in every aspect of a customer project. They contribute ideas in the quoting stage and support design changes and product launches (PPAP); and are responsible for control plans, PFMEA, gauge requirements, capability studies, material standards, and outside supplier qualification.

Critical Supply Chain Management integrates our raw material suppliers with our manufacturing processes to reduce inventory and lead time. Our logistics team is committed to continuously improving customer response. Planning and scheduling systems are designed to provide 100% on-time delivery.
