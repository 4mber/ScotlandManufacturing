New Additions

In May of 2017, Matt Barnhardt was brought on board in the newly-created position of Supply Chain Manager. Matt has extensive experience in production scheduling, purchasing and logistics, and will bring these skills to Scotland Manufacturing in order to better serve our Customers and meet our on- time delivery goals. Welcome to our team, Matt!

In July of 2017, William Locklear was hired in the position of Quality Manager. William is a Laurinburg native who wanted to return to his hometown roots. William also has valuable quality engineering experience and is versed in ISO 9001:2015. We’re excited to have William join our team!

Safety – Our #1 Goal!

Scotland Manufacturing believes that the safety of all employees is of the utmost importance. In May of 2017, Scotland celebrated 5 years without a lost-time accident. To mark the occasion, employees were treated to a steak lunch and served by management. We are dedicated to making Scotland a safe work environment, now and always.
