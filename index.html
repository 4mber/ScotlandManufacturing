• Scotland Manufacturing has the capability to produce products for a wide range of industries including filtration, automotive, construction, rail and aerospace.

  • Deep drawn filter shells are manufactured to meet the tough standards required by the heavy-duty market.

  • For the agricultural and earth moving equipment industries, Scotland Manufacturing produces large stampings such as door panels, hoods and fenders.

  • Scotland Manufacturing's expert production team can create products to exact requirements from simple designs to more intricate shapes.

  • A unique step in the design of this filter shell, for the heavy-trucking industry, allows the shells to be stacked which reduces shipping costs.

  • For the automotive market, Scotland Manufacturing's capabilities include door panels for the high-end automotive OEM, clutch plates, transmission plates, brackets, and filter cans.

  • Filter cans are carefully inspected and securely packaged before shipping to a customer.

  • For the construction industry, Scotland Manufacturing produces components such as chimney caps, and brackets and braces for prefabricated buildings.

  • Chimney cover caps are used in both commercial and residential applications.

  • A variety of products is manufactured on Scotland Manufacturing's 600-ton automated transfer press.

  • Scotland Manufacturing can fabricate components for fire extinguishers from the highest quality materials in a short lead time.

  • This filter is a mandatory requirement for fire extinguishers used in the aerospace industry.

  • Scotland Manufacturing offers value-added services such as welding, assembly and pressure testing.

  • Scotland Manufacturing's capabilities for the rail industry include brake intercasings, shoes and pads, and friction products.

  • Inner cases for rail car brakes allow the brakes to function properly.

Cold Rolled Steel Experts

Since 1979, Scotland Manufacturing met the needs of the world's premier original equipment and component manufacturers by providing high-quality products and services. Working as a proactive partner with its customers, the company offers creative solutions for product development and fabrication.

Scotland Manufacturing — Experts in Deep Drawn Stampings Using Cold Rolled Steel

Our Commitment to Excellence - We will meet and exceed customer expectations safely and profitably, while seeking continuous improvement in quality, cost and delivery.

Our Mission

Scotland Manufacturing is dedicated to providing excellent value and service in a broad range of drawn, stamped and fabricated components. We take pride in being proactively involved with our customers — meeting customer requirements by learning about their business, product lines, scheduling needs, and business goals.

1300 Days of Safety

Scotland Manufacturing is entering 2017 with an outstanding record - over 1300 days with no lost-time accidents. Through extensive safety training and strict adherence to safety guidelines, all Scotland Manufacturing employees perform their work safely, in a safe environment. We are proud of this accomplishment and continue to encourage safe practices in our facility.
